Bang Bang Sauce

Description

This bang bang sauce is a sweet and spicy mayonnaise-based sauce that is good on fried fish, chicken, fried potatoes

â€” almost everything!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons sweet Thai Chili Sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Sriracha Sauce
  • 1 teaspoon rice vinegar

Directions

  1. Stir together mayonnaise, chili sauce, Sriracha, and rice vinegar in a small bowl until smooth.
